### Course Description
Under the class of Innovation 5.0, Web3 marketing is the focal discipline challenging students to create a Web3 marketing campaign for an established brand within a very limited budget and high ROI expectations.

The second part of the course is focused on identifying and analyzing emerging consumer, cultural, and technological trends that shape markets. Students will learn methodologies for spotting trends early, assess their potential impact on industries, and develop strategic responses for brands to stay ahead of the curve.

Students will be equipped with the skills to turn emerging trends into actionable marketing strategies that drive innovation and business growth.

### Key Web3 Marketing Concepts

**NFTs (Non-Fungible Tokens)**
- Unique digital assets on blockchain
- Used for digital art, collectibles, membership access
- Can be used for token-gating exclusive content

**Metaverse**
- Virtual worlds where users can interact
- Marketing opportunities: virtual stores, events, brand experiences
- Examples: Decentraland, The Sandbox, Spatial

**Token-Gating**
- Restricting access to content/community based on token ownership
- Creates exclusive communities
- Used for: Discord servers, content access, events

**DAO (Decentralized Autonomous Organization)**
- Community-governed organizations
- Token holders vote on decisions
- Alternative to traditional corporate structures

**Phygital**
- Combination of physical and digital experiences
- Example: Physical product with NFT counterpart
- Bridge between Web2 and Web3

**Hype Marketing**
- Creating scarcity and FOMO (Fear Of Missing Out)
- Limited drops, exclusive access
- Examples: Supreme, Nike drops, NFT projects like Azuki

**Flywheel Effect**
- Self-reinforcing marketing cycle
- Community growth → More value → More members → More growth
- Key to sustainable Web3 marketing

**Web3 Marketing Funnel**
- Awareness (Social media, influencers)
- Consideration (Community engagement, token utility)
- Conversion (Token purchase, NFT mint)
- Retention (Token-gated benefits, community rewards)
- Advocacy (Community members become marketers)

## PRIORITY 2 - KEY CONCEPTS

### Innovation 5.0 Definition
Innovation 5.0 represents the convergence of Web3 technologies with marketing strategy, emphasizing:
- Decentralization
- Community-first approach
- Token-based economies
- Digital ownership
- Metaverse integration

### Web3 vs Web2 Marketing

| Aspect | Web2 | Web3 |
|--------|------|------|
| Ownership | Platform owns data | User owns data/assets |
| Community | Followers | Token holders/DAO members |
| Monetization | Ads, subscriptions | Tokens, NFTs, royalties |
| Trust | Platform-dependent | Blockchain-verified |
| Engagement | Likes, shares | Governance, staking |

### Next-Gen Loyalty Programs
- Token-based rewards
- NFT membership cards
- Community governance rights
- Exclusive token-gated benefits
- Tradable loyalty points
